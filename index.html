Frequently Asked Questions

1. How can I start learning English on this website?
You can start by exploring our beginner lessons, interactive exercises, and quizzes. We also offer structured courses to guide you step by step.
2. Is this website free to use?
Yes, our basic lessons and vocabulary practice are completely free. Some advanced courses or features may require registration to access.
3. Do I need to create an account?
Creating an account is not required to explore basic lessons. However, signing up helps you track your progress and access more features.
4. How can I build my English vocabulary?
You can improve your vocabulary by practicing daily lessons, listening to pronunciations, and reviewing new words regularly through our interactive vocabulary cards.
5. Do you offer certificates for completed courses?
No, we currently do not provide certificates for completed courses. Our main goal is to help learners improve their English skills through practical lessons and vocabulary practice.
